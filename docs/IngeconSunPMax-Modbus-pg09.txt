30116 1000V Kit usage Counter - _Y 
30117 Power reduction register 
- Bit 0: Power limitation on (The inverter is not injecting all the available 
solar power). 
- Bit 1: Modbus power reduction on. 
- Bit 2: Max frequency power reduction on. 
- Bit 3: Grid fault power reduction on. 
- Bit 4: High Vdc power reduction on. 
- Bit 5: High temperature power reduction on. 
- _Z
