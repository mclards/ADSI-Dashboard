## Slide 2: Project Overview

The ADSI Inverter Dashboard brings key plant information into one clear view. It helps teams monitor generation, review performance, respond to issues, and prepare reports more efficiently.

**Main Highlights**

- Live view of plant and inverter status
- Daily and historical performance review
- Day-ahead energy forecasting
- Alarm monitoring and action history
- Safer standby refresh with explicit overwrite confirmation
- Controlled restart and update install flow
- CSV and Excel export for reporting

## Slide 3: Main Dashboard

Provides a central view of real-time plant output, daily generation, inverter status, and fast inverter control for quick operational response. The inverter cards use a compact live summary layout with clearer titles, readable PAC values, and fixed-status legend colors for better wide-screen operations use.

## Slide 4: Navigation Menu

Offers simple access to the main sections of the system, including monitoring, analytics, alarms, reports, exports, and settings.

## Slide 5: Analytics Dashboard

Shows production trends and historical performance to support better operating decisions.

## Slide 6: Energy Generation Tracker

Displays interval-based energy records to help validate production and review daily results.

## Slide 7: Inverter Performance Review

Compares inverter performance to quickly identify underperforming units and unusual operating conditions. Card-level `Pdc` and `Pac` summaries are kept visually above the node table so fast operator scanning stays efficient.

## Slide 8: Alarms and Alerts

Highlights important issues that require attention while reducing nuisance audio from short transient alarms, helping teams respond faster and reduce downtime.

## Slide 9: Operations History

Keeps a clear record of operating actions for review, traceability, and accountability.

## Slide 10: Day-Ahead Forecasting

Solcast forecasts support export-ready day-ahead scheduling and intra-day renomination updates, enabling timely market submission and more accurate operational planning.

## Slide 11: Data Export

Allows users to export clean CSV and Excel reports for operations, reporting, and management review.

## Slide 12: Settings Center

Provides a single place to manage plant setup, connectivity, low-impact standby refresh, controlled restart/update installs, and other system options.
