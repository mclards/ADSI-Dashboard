For hybrid plants where Etotal grows after midnight (grid feed-in / storage discharge), there's a tiny attribution shift: tomorrow's baseline is seeded at 00:01 from yesterday's eClean as it stood at that instant, not from the eClean's final 04:55 value. Any growth between 00:01 and 04:55 lives on yesterday's row (per productionDayKey), so yesterday's energy is correctly accounted for; today just opens with a slightly older anchor. For your pure-solar plant this is not observable (Etotal is frozen all night).
